Welcome to Penang!

Wide variety of amazing food and picture perfect sunsets.

Chew Jetty

Walk along the dock and even take a boat ride if you're interested! There are a few stalls along the walk to the jetty, where you can purchase trinkets, handmade crafts and homemade ice cream.

Raja Uda

This stretch is lit up with lanters especially during Chinese New Year. Hawker stalls are present everywhere you turn, where you can get the best delicasies of Penang. Most favoured dishes include Char Koay Teow, and Penang Hokkien Mee (although in Penang it's just known as Hokkien Mee).
